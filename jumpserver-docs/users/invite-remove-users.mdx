---
title: "Invite or remove user from an organization"
---

In the Enterprise edition, you can invite a user from one organization to another and assign them specific roles. The user will automatically inherit the permissions associated with those roles in the new organization.

Removing a user from an organization will revoke their access but will not delete the user.

## Bulk invite users to the current organization

<Steps>

<Step>
In the left area of the top navigation bar, click the organization dropdown and switch to the target organization.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/docs/navs/switch-org.png" alt="Switch organization" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step>
At the top-left of the page, select <Icon icon="arrow-left-right" />, then click <Icon icon="desktop" /> **Console**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the left menu, click **<Icon icon="user" /> Users**.
</Step>

<Step>
Above the user table, Click **Invite**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the **Users** field, select one or more users to invite.
</Step>

<Step>
In the **Org roles** field, select one or more organization roles.
</Step>

<Step>
Click **Confirm**.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Remove a user from the current organization

<Steps>

<Step>
In the left area of the top navigation bar, click the organization dropdown and switch to the target organization.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/docs/navs/switch-org.png" alt="Switch organization" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step>
At the top-left of the page, select <Icon icon="bars" />, then click <Icon icon="desktop" /> **Console**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the left menu, click **<Icon icon="user" /> Users**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the user table, find the user you want to remove.
</Step>

<Step>
In the Actions column, click the **···** icon, then click **Remove**.
</Step>

<Step>
Confirm the information and click **OK**.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Bulk remove users from the current organization

<Steps>

<Step>
In the left area of the top navigation bar, click the organization dropdown and switch to the target organization.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/docs/navs/switch-org.png" alt="Switch organization" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step>
At the top-left of the page, select <Icon icon="bars" />, then click <Icon icon="desktop" /> **Console**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the left menu, click **<Icon icon="user" /> Users**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the user table, check the users you want to remove.
</Step>

<Step>
Above the user table, select **Actions <Icon icon="chevron-down" />** button, then click **<Icon icon="minus-circle" /> Remove selected**.
</Step>

</Steps>
