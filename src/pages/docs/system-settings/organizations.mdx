# Organizations

This topic describes about Organiztions settings of JumpServer.

## Create an organization

1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > Organizations**.

3. Click the **+ Create** button above the Org. list.
  
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/create-organization.png" alt="org-create-button" width="325"/>

- Alternatively, Click **Create Oraganiztion** in **Org. Selecter** on navbar.

  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/create-organization-nav.png" alt="org-create-button-nav" width="325"/>

4. In the **Name** field, enter the organization name.
5. Optionally, in the **Description** field, enter a description of the organization.
6. Click **Submit** to create the organization.

## View Organization details 

1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > organizations**.
3. In the Organiztions list, locate the relevant org and click the Org. name to open the details page.
4. On the `Basic` page (default), where you can view `Basic information` and Activities.

<img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/organization-detail.png" alt="org-detail" width="550"/>

## Edit organization properties and information 

1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > organizations**.
3. In the Organizations list, locate the relevant Org. and click the **Edit** in the `Actions` column. Alternatively, in the top-right corner of the Org. details page, click **Edit**.
4. Change the properties or information that you want, and then click **Submit**.

## Delete an organization

1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > organizations**.
3. In the Orgnaizations list, locate the relevant Org. and select the **···** dropdown menu in the Actions column, then click **Delete**. **Make sure User, User group, Asset, Node, Label, Zone, Authorization within the Org. have been deleted first**.
4. Confirm the information to be deleted in the popup, and then click **OK**.

## Duplicate an organization

1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > organizations**.
3. In the Organizations list, locate the relevant Org. and select the **···** dropdown menu in the Actions column, then click **Duplicate**.
4. Modify the user properties and information and Then click **Submit**.

##  View Organization activities

1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > organizations**.

3. In the Organizations list, locate the relevant Org. and click the Org. name to open the details page.

4. On the `Activities` page, you can view the last 30 activity logs of the Org..

## Rename Global organization
1. In the upper-right corner of any page in JumpServer, click <img src="/images/docs/svg/nav-setting.svg" alt="x" width="15" style={{ display: "inline-block" }}/>.
  <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/nav-settings.png" alt="image" width="450"/>

2. Navigate to **System settings > organizations**.

3. Click the **Settings** button above the Organizations list.
  
<img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/rename-global-organization.png" alt="rename-global-org" width="320"/>

4. In the **Global organization** field, enter the organization name.

5. Click **Submit** to rename the global organization.
