# Organizations <Icon name="xpackTip"/>

This topic describes about Organizations settings of JumpServer.

## About Organizations

JumpServer supports **Organizations management**, where users, assets, and permissions are isolated from different organizations. 
This makes it convenient for administrators to manage and view information within different Org. based on the company's real Org. structure.

User can switch **Organizations** through Org. selector in the upper-left corner in JumpServer.
<img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/organization.png" alt="image" width="350"/>

## Create an organization

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
Navigate to **System settings > Organizations**.
</div>

<div>
Click the **+ Create** button above the Org. list.
<img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/create-organization.png" alt="org-create-button" width="325"/>
- Alternatively, Click **Create Organizations** in **Org. Select** on navbar.
<img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/system-settings/organizations/create-organization-nav.png" alt="org-create-button" width="325"/>
</div>

<div>
In the **Name** field, enter the organization name.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, in the **Description** field, enter a description of the organization.
</div>

<div>
Click **Submit**.
</div>

</Steps>

## Edit organization properties and information 

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
Navigate to **System settings > Organizations**.
</div>

<div>
In the Organizations list, locate the relevant Org. and click the **Edit** in the `Actions` column. Alternatively, in the top-right corner of the Org. details page, click **Edit**.
</div>

<div>
Change the properties or information that you want, and then click **Submit**.
</div> 

</Steps>


## Delete an organization

<Steps>
<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
Navigate to **System settings > Organizations**.
</div>

<div>
In the Organizations list, locate the relevant Org. and select the **···** dropdown menu in the Actions column, then click **Delete**. **Make sure User, User group, Asset, Node, Label, Zone, Authorization within the Org. have been deleted first**.
</div>

<div>
Confirm the information to be deleted in the popup, and then click **OK**.
</div>
</Steps>
