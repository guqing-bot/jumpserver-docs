## Enable or disable a user

<Steps>

<Step>
At the top-left of the page, select <Icon icon="arrow-left-right" />, then click <b>Console</b>.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/docs/navs/nav-console.png" alt="image" width="300"/>
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step>
In the left menu, click **<Icon icon="user" /> Users**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the user table, click the name of the user you want to enable or disable.
</Step>

<Step>
On the user details page, click **Basic** tab.
</Step>

<Step>
In the Quick update card, turn on/off the "Active" toggle to **enable** or **disable** the user.
</Step>

</Steps>

## Bulk enable or disable users

<Steps>

<Step>
At the top-left of the page, select <Icon icon="arrow-left-right" />, then click <b>Console</b>.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/docs/navs/nav-console.png" alt="image" width="300"/>
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step>
In the left menu, click **<Icon icon="user" /> Users**.
</Step>

<Step>
In the user table, check the users you want to enable or disable.
</Step>

<Step>
Above the table, select **Actions <Icon icon="chevron-down" />** button, then click **<Icon icon="circle-check" /> Activate selected** or **<Icon icon="ban" /> Disable selected**.
</Step>

</Steps>
